Frequently Asked Questions

  • What wetsuit thickness do I need for a specific water temperature?
    The appropriate wetsuit thickness varies based on water temperature. Here's a general guide:
     • 2mm-3mm for warm water (above 70°F / 21°C)
     • 3mm-5mm for cool water (60-70°F / 16-21°C)
     • 5mm-7mm for cold water (below 60°F / 16°C)
  • Can I return or exchange a wetsuit if it doesn't fit?
    Yes, we offer a hassle-free return and exchange policy. Please ensure the wetsuit is unworn, with tags attached, and returned within 30 days of purchase.
  • What's the difference between fiberglass and foam surfboards?
    Fiberglass boards are typically more durable and offer better performance, suited for intermediate to advanced surfers. Foam boards are ideal for beginners due to their buoyancy and forgiving nature.
  • Do you offer swim gear for children?
    Absolutely! Sellers offer a wide range of swim gear for kids, including wetsuits, swim fins, goggles, and more, tailored to their sizes and needs.
  • How should I care for my swim gear to ensure longevity?
    Rinse your gear with fresh water after each use to remove salt and sand. Avoid direct sunlight exposure and store equipment properly in a cool, dry place. For specific care instructions, refer to the product manual or contact our customer service team.
  • Can you recommend the best gear for open water swimming?
    For open water swimming, we suggest a combination of a buoyancy aid, high-visibility swim cap, goggles with UV protection, and a wetsuit suitable for the water temperature.
  • What's the advantage of neoprene wetsuits over lycra?
    Neoprene wetsuits offer better insulation and protection against colder water, whereas lycra suits are more lightweight and suitable for warmer conditions.

    Yes, most of our products come with a manufacturer's warranty. Please check the specific product details for warranty information.
  • Do you offer any discounts or package deals?
